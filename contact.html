Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to make a bespoke garment?

In short, we recommend allowing at least 2 and at most 8 weeks before the first fitting. This timeframe may vary depending on the availability of your chosen materials and the workload of your chosen tailor. If you have requested a specific tailor, their availability may also affect the timeline. If you have a specific deadline in mind, please let us know as soon as possible so we can work together to ensure your garment is completed on time. We always strive to deliver high-quality garments within a reasonable timeframe.

How many fittings do I need for a bespoke garment?

We typically schedule one to two fittings for each garment to ensure that it fits you perfectly. However, there may be some specific situations where additional fittings may be required. For instance, if you have significant changes in your body size or shape during the tailoring process, we may need to schedule additional fittings to make necessary adjustments.

Do you offer alterations on non-Gar De'Roba garments?

Yes, we offer alterations on non-Gar De'Roba garments. However, please keep in mind that the extent of the alterations we can make may be limited. For example, it is generally easier to remove material from an item than it is to make a complete garment bigger. We will do our best to make the desired alterations, but we cannot guarantee the same level of quality as with our own bespoke garments.

Can I request a specific fabric for my garment?

Ofcourse, we're happy to source any fabric on behalf of our customers just be mindful that it might delay the completion of your garment. It's best to notify your tailor about it at the first consultation.
